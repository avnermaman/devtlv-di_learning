  • Description

    Want to become a web developer? This is how it’s done. In this intensive, fully immersive, English-speaking course, we'll teach you everything you need to know to unlock your spot in one of the fastest-growing industries in Israel. Don’t worry if you don't have previous experience - beginners are welcome. Our program will provide you with mentorship, a data-driven curriculum and a top notch learning environment that will launch you into your first developer role. Your work starts with our prep course (2 weeks duration) before the course officially starts. On day one, we hit full speed and don’t stop until you graduate.

    Learn from experts: Receive instruction and mentorship from top web developers and connect with Developers.Institute's thriving community of pros and peers. Develop new skills: build real websites while collaborating with classmates on a range of projects.

    What you’ll learn

    We teach the skills you need to kick-start your career as a developer and a lifelong learner. From programming fundamentals to launching full-stack web apps, you'll learn to solve problems with code while applying industry best practices in a collaborative environment. Our immersive Full-stack Python course emphasizes the Python coding language.  We chose to focus on Python due to its speed and analytical capabilities, while also being simple. Python has consistently been ranked the highest paid coding language for recent bootcamp grads by Course Report.

    Requirements

    • Beginners accepted
    • Any computer will work: Windows, macOS or Linux

    Unlimited Bootcamps

    The world of programming is never ending. New technologies are constantly being crafted and you, as a future developer, need to keep up with such advancements. This is why we offer lifetime access to our courses both onsite and online. We call it Unlimited Bootcamp. With this unique value-added benefit, graduates of Developers.Institute  have access to our courses for life. Graduates can return at any time to learn a new technology or refresh their expertise on an existing technology— and in most cases there is no charge whatsoever. It’s a free software upgrade for your knowledge and skills.

    Career Guidance

    Master new skills and put them to work. All of our immersive graduates receive a career coach, job-readiness training, and direct access to employers and professionals. Job & career coaching Connection to resources Networking opportunities Our in-house career coaches provide resources and guidance to get you on track for an exciting career.

    Mindset Classes

    Throughout the bootcamp, we offer extra-curricular lectures and workshops to help you learn the startup mindset, prepare for job searches, develop entrepreneur thought processes and more. These are all optional, but highly recommended!

    Experience

    After completing a course, we offer our graduates help securing an optional 2-month (unpaid) internship, so that you will be able to add real-world experience to your CV.

    • Michael DeutschFull Stack Coding Bootcamp - Python
      "Developers Institute is a Bootcamp that Cares"

      The very best thing about attending Developers Institute is how much the founders truly care about the satisfaction of all attendees. In addition to "mandatory" reviews every week, asking what was good and what could be improved upon, whenever anyone came with a specific request or comment, there was a desire to implement it as soon as possible.

      Overall, I do think some people begin a bootcamp with an unclear or unrealistic idea about what to expect, and how much a bootcamp can help them getting started in a job in the development field. A bootcamp WILL NOT simply teach you to code by merely attending. You still need to put in tons of work during the course (and possibly afterwards) to get your skills up to a strong level. Whether you attend a bootcamp or not, YOU still have to do a lot of coding and learning to gain experience. However, Developers Institute does a great job of showing you the path, helping you take the steps along it, and correcting you if you're making mistakes. Don't think that just finishing the course will automatically mean you'll find a job right away. After a short internship that I did which was set up by Developers Institute, I received a job offer as a full stack developer.

      So, do your research. Understand the balance between what a bootcamp provides, and the work and effort you need to commit. And if you decide a bootcamp is for you, Developers Institute is a great choice!
